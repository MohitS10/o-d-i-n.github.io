What We Do

ODIN Strives For

Software Development

Real World Problems are dealt with Softwares, not Programs. Thus we aim to break the "coding" stereotype at NSIT so as to create an understanding about industry standards and practices.

Collaborations

We promote community driven development and to achieve the same, we provide a platform for students to build and contribute to various open-sourced projects.

Knowledge

Everything is available to everyone when it comes to open-source. What better way to learn and share!
